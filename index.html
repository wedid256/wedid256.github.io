About Us

Liam Generation Limited is an Engineering firm specializing in civil engineering projects and supplies. Our expertise encompasses construction, development of structural designs and architectural plans, structural maintenance, and project management. Additionally, we have other established departments dedicated to providing comprehensive services in real estate development, land and quantity surveying.
